## Client's Goal:

The goal for Wisdom was to create a mobile app that enriches users' daily lives with motivational and thought-provoking quotes. The app aims to offer a wide range of quotes that cater to various interests and inspire personal growth and reflection.

## Skailex's Contribution:

Our agency, was responsible for designing and developing the Wisdom app. We focused on creating an engaging and user-friendly interface, incorporating a diverse range of quotes, and ensuring a seamless experience for users to receive daily inspirational notifications.

## Project Success:

Wisdom has been well-received, with users appreciating the vast collection of quotes and the convenience of receiving daily inspirational messages. The app's success is evident in its user engagement and positive feedback.

## Skills:

Mobile App Design, UI/UI Prototyping, React Native, TypeScript, Next.js, TypeScript, Node.js, Vercel, React
