Wisdom is a mobile app that delivers quotes to your daily life. There are 5 categories: Success, Inspiration, Wisdom, Friendship, and Famous Quotes. With over +4000 quotes available in the app and daily notifications to inspire you, Wisdom helps you get through each day with life lessons and thoughts from everyone around the globe who has been successful in their own way.

## Features

- Inspirational quotes
- Daily reminders
- Beautiful native widgets
- Sharing with audience
